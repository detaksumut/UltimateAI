# Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) Concept

## UltimateAI

### Building AI-Powered Research Infrastructure for the Global Scientific Community

---

## Background

UltimateAI is an initiative to develop an artificial intelligence platform designed to help researchers create research software, scientific information systems, repositories, analytical dashboards, and various digital research tools without requiring programming skills.

The project is being developed as an open-source platform that supports digital transformation in research, Open Science, and the advancement of global research infrastructure.

---

## Purpose of Collaboration

This collaboration aims to:

1. Develop UltimateAI as an AI-powered research software generation platform.
2. Foster collaboration among researchers, academics, software developers, and institutions.
3. Support the growth of Open Science and open research infrastructure.
4. Build a more inclusive and accessible research ecosystem.
5. Accelerate the transformation of research ideas into deployable software solutions.

---

## Shared Vision

To create a future where every researcher can build the research software they need without becoming a programmer.

## Guiding Principles

All parties agree that:

1. UltimateAI is developed to advance scientific knowledge and research innovation.
2. UltimateAI embraces Open Science principles.
3. UltimateAI promotes interdisciplinary collaboration.
4. UltimateAI respects intellectual property rights and contributor recognition.
5. UltimateAI supports transparency, openness, sustainability, and community-driven development.

---

## Project Governance

UltimateAI will adopt a governance model based on:

* Open Source Governance
* Community Collaboration
* Scientific Integrity
* Transparency
* Sustainability

Strategic decisions will be made through mutually agreed governance mechanisms involving key stakeholders and contributors.

---

## Ownership and Licensing

UltimateAI is developed as an open-source project.

Source code, documentation, and technological developments will be governed by the project's selected open-source license while respecting the contributions and rights of all participants.

---

## Long-Term Objective

To establish UltimateAI as a global platform that enables researchers, universities, laboratories, research centers, and scientific institutions to create their own research software using artificial intelligence.
